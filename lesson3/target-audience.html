Persona 1

George Bailey
  • Occupation: Farmer
  • Demographics and Education: 45 years old, Agriculture degree.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Predicting rain, snow, and when to plant, harvest, and fertilize his crops.
  • Social: Spends time at the feed store with the other farmers occasionally. Usually too busy for social activities.
  • Technology: Single phone, used to communicate with wife, farmhands, and other farmers.
  • Quote: The most memorable days usually end with the dirtiest clothes.

Persona 2

Suzanne Howe
  • Occupation: Homemaker.
  • Demographics and Education: 35 years old, Bachelors Degree in Marriage and Family.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Planning and organizing her family adventures around the weekly forecast.
  • Social: Loves being with her family outdoors, skiing, hiking, and travel for family reunions in Utah.
  • Technology: Extensive. Uses her phone to keep track of activities, family, and church. Has Ipad for distracting cranky children. Has a digital camera for picture taking of the kids to share with extended family.
  • Quote: Families are Forever.
