PARC: Repetition

LowCarb with Jennifer

The repetition found on this site brings unity to the design and strengthens the visual appeal. Not only is the design element repeated, but the spaces between them as well. This creates a sense of movement and cohesiveness.

White space and Clean Design

Lil Luna

This site is a great example of white space. It is a clean website with a simple interface. By putting the lower icons on a white or transparent background, it elevates the design and ensures that it does not feel cluttered.

Visual Hierarchy

American Red Cross

The use of visual hierarchy is shown in the bright red button that emphasizes the ability to make your appointment to donate blood. It is both an attention getter and a call to action for the person viewing the website.
