The Mountains in Fish Haven Festival

By Joann Casper


If you've ever been to Redfish Lake, chances are you've seen the Grand Mogul since it is the most prominent peak visible over the lake. It is located in the Sawtooth Mountains and has a summit of 9,733 feet.

As darkness approached and a lone bull elk below me quit bugling, clusters of lights to the south began revealing the locations of a half-dozen Magic Valley towns.


If snow is a bit scarce, there will still be a long list of fun activities for families to enjoy including ice sculptures, food trucks, a bonfire hosted by members of the Fish Haven Fire Department, a puppet show provided by Fish Haven Public Library, visits with princesses from the Disney movie "Frozen," and visits with Frosty the snowman.


There will also be fun Harry Potter-themed activities available, and a Fish Haven King and Queen will be crowned. Clowns will be on hand to show off some fun balloon art and families can also enjoy a petting zoo.
